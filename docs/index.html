Ackerman Negotiation Model

Based on FBI hostage negotiation tactics from "Never Split the Difference" by Chris Voss.

65%
Round 1

Anchor low with tactical empathy. Use precise numbers like $387.

85%
Round 2

Mirror their response. Add value: "Cash pickup in 1 hour".

95%
Round 3

Label their situation. Create urgency. Show flexibility.

100%
Round 4

Walk-away bluff. "Totally understand if it doesn't work!"
